Establishing the Frequency

THE ARCHITECT OF DARK SOUNDS.

Forged in the late-night sessions of industrial basement studios, VNTG_STUDIO translates the static of the city into rhythmic precision. We don't just make music; we engineer sonic environments that breathe and pulse.

NARRATIVE.

My journey began at the intersection of classical training and digital rebellion. After a decade of exploring the fringes of techno and ambient soundscapes, I established VNTG_STUDIO as a laboratory for auditory experimentation. Each track is a meticulous assembly of found sounds, analog warmth, and surgical digital processing.

Philosophy

"Sound is a physical sculpture. My job is to find the shapes hidden in the noise."

Process

Hybrid workflow utilizing vintage hardware from the 80s paired with custom-built neural processing units.
